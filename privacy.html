Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

FuelDiary ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, license plate number, VIN, fuel type, and tank capacity
  • Fuel Records: Date, odometer reading, fuel quantity, cost, location, and notes
  • Trip Information: Trip title, start/end dates, locations, waypoints, purposes, and notes
  • Expenses: Date, amount, category, description, and associated receipts
  • Service Records: Service type, date, cost, odometer reading, and reminders
  • Documents: Photos and scanned documents you choose to store in the app
  • Photos: Trip cover photos and receipt images

Data Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you enable trip tracking (only while app is in use)
  • Usage Data: App features you use and interaction patterns (stored locally only)
  • Device Information: iOS version and device model (for compatibility purposes)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Tracking vehicle fuel consumption and expenses
  • Calculating fuel efficiency and statistics
  • Managing trip records and locations
  • Providing AI-powered maintenance predictions
  • Organizing service reminders
  • Generating reports and analytics
  • Enabling Siri Shortcuts functionality

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted SwiftData framework. We do NOT store your data on external servers, upload it to the cloud (except your personal iCloud), share it with third parties, or track your usage across apps.

Security Measures

  • Biometric Protection: Sensitive data (license plates, documents) requires Face ID or Touch ID
  • Encrypted Storage: All data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption
  • No Accounts Required: No email, phone number, or personal identifiers needed

Location Services

We only access your location when you start a trip with GPS tracking enabled and while the app is actively in use. This data is used for recording routes, geocoding names (on-device), and calculating distances. It is never sent to external servers.

Photos and Camera

We access your camera or photo library only when you select photos for trip covers or scan receipts. Images are stored locally, compressed, and OCR processing is done on-device using VisionKit and Apple Foundation Models. No images are ever uploaded.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data. You can access, export as CSV/PDF/JSON, or delete individual records anytime. Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently.
