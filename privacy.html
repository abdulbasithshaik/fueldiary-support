Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Introduction

FuelDiary ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, license plate, VIN, fuel type.
  • Fuel Records: Date, odometer, quantity, cost, location, notes.
  • Trip Information: Routes, dates, waypoints, notes.
  • Expenses & Service Records.
  • Photos & Documents.

Data Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (only during active trip tracking).
  • Usage Data: Interaction patterns (stored locally).
  • Device Information: iOS version, model.

How We Use Your Data

  • Tracking consumption and expenses.
  • Calculating efficiency statistics.
  • Maintenance predictions (AI).
  • Service reminders.
  • Generating reports.

Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's encrypted SwiftData framework.

We do NOT:

  • Store data on external servers.
  • Upload data to the cloud (except your personal iCloud).
  • Share data with third parties.
  • Track usage across apps/websites.

Location Services

We access location only when you start a trip with GPS tracking enabled and the app is in use.

  • Used for recording routes and calculating distances.
  • Reverse geocoding is processed on-device.
  • You can disable location access in iOS Settings.

AI, Photos & Camera

Photos are stored locally and compressed. OCR processing is done on-device using VisionKit.

AI maintenance predictions are processed locally. No data is sent to external AI services.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all data in-app.
  • Export: CSV, PDF, JSON formats.
  • Delete: Remove records or vehicles anytime.
  • Opt-Out: Disable GPS or Biometrics in Settings.
