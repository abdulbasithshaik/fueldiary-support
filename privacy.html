Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

FuelDiary respects your privacy. Our core philosophy is that your data belongs to you. Unlike other apps, we don't store your vehicle logs on our servers.

Data We Collect Locally

  • Vehicle Info: Make, Model, License Plates (encrypted).
  • Fuel & Trips: Odometer, quantities, and GPS routes.
  • Photos: Receipts and trip images (processed locally via Apple Foundation Models).

100% Privacy Guarantee

Local Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We NEVER:

  • Upload your logs to our servers
  • Sell your data to advertisers
  • Track your behavior for analytics

Location Usage

Location access is only used when you explicitly start a "Trip" tracker. This data is used solely to generate your trip map and distance statistics on-device.

Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please reach out to support@fueldiary.app.
