Frequently Asked Questions

How do Reports work?

The Reports tab provides deep analysis. We automatically find your vehicle's odometer baseline to ensure stats are 100% accurate, even with sparse data.

How does Receipt OCR work?

Tap 'Scan Receipt' to capture your receipt. The app features dynamic currency detection, automatically identifying $, ‚Ç¨, ¬£, and other local symbols based on your settings.

What are AI Insights?

AI Insights predict upcoming service dates and refueling needs based on your driving patterns. This processing happens entirely on your device.

Where is my data stored?

Fuel Diary respects your privacy. All your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud. We do not have access to your maps, routes, or logs.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. Choose CSV, PDF, or JSON format. Share the file via email, cloud storage, or any other app on your iPhone.

What documents should I store?

We recommend storing: Driving License, Vehicle Registration, Insurance Policy, and Service Records. These are protected with biometric authentication.
