Frequently Asked Questions

How do Reports work?

The Reports tab (üìä) provides deep analysis. We've refined the logic to automatically find your vehicle's odometer baseline just before the selected period, ensuring 'Last Month' and 'Last 3 Months' stats are 100% accurate, even with sparse data.

Can I filter my trips?

Yes! In the 'All Trips' screen, you can use the side-by-side filters at the top to filter by specific Vehicle and Category (Personal, Business, etc.). You can also toggle to see only trips with GPS recorded paths.

How do I use Siri Shortcuts?

Just say 'Hey Siri' followed by a command like 'Add fuel log in FuelDiary', 'Start trip', or 'Get vehicle status'. No setup required! Visit Shortcuts app to see all available commands.

How does Receipt OCR work?

In Add Fuel Log, tap 'Scan Receipt' to capture your receipt. The app now features dynamic currency detection, automatically identifying $, ‚Ç¨, ¬£, and other local symbols based on your settings. (Pro)

What are AI Insights?

AI Insights predict upcoming service dates and refueling needs based on your driving patterns. Find them on the Vehicle Detail screen. Requires at least 7 days of data and is a Pro feature.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. Choose CSV (free), PDF (Pro), or JSON (Pro) format. Share the file via email, cloud storage, or any app. Perfect for backups or tax records.

How do I edit a fuel log?

Navigate to your vehicle details, find the fuel log you want to edit, and swipe left. Tap the 'Edit' button to modify the details. You can also delete logs by swiping left and tapping 'Delete'.

What's the difference between expenses and fuel logs?

Fuel logs specifically track fuel purchases and calculate efficiency metrics. Expenses are for other costs like tolls, parking, car washes, etc. Both are tracked separately but contribute to your total vehicle costs.

How do I delete a vehicle?

Go to the vehicle's detail page, scroll to the bottom, and tap 'Delete Vehicle'. Note: This will permanently delete all associated data including fuel logs, trips, expenses, and documents. This action cannot be undone.

What are trips and how do they work?

Trips help you organize journeys by tracking start/end dates, odometer readings, and categories (Personal, Business, Vacation). Create a trip, log fuel and expenses during it, then close it to see complete statistics. Trips are great for tracking specific journeys.

How do Live Activities work?

Start a trip and lock your phone to see live speed, distance, and duration. For a professional dashboard, mount your iPhone in landscape while charging to trigger the Standby Trip Computer. (Pro)

What documents should I store?

We recommend storing: Driving License, Vehicle Registration, Insurance Policy, Emission Certificate, and Service Records. Sensitive documents (license, registration, insurance) are automatically protected with biometric authentication.
