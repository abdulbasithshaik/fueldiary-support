Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's private secure servers.

Can I backup my records?

Yes! Use the 'Export Data' feature in the More tab to save a JSON backup of everything. We also recommend standard iOS iCloud backups.
